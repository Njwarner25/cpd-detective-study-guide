## ‚úÖ **Solution 1: Check Your Internet Connection**

- Make sure you have a stable WiFi or mobile data connection
- Try opening other websites (like google.com) to verify your connection works
- If on mobile data, check that you have good signal strength
- Try switching between WiFi and mobile data

## ‚úÖ **Solution 2: Clear Your Browser Cache**

### For Android/Chrome:
1. Tap the **three dots** menu (‚ãÆ) in the top-right corner
2. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Privacy** ‚Üí **Clear browsing data**
3. Select **"Cached images and files"**
4. Tap **"Clear data"**
5. Close and reopen the browser
6. Visit the app again

### For iPhone/Safari:
1. Open **Settings** app
2. Scroll down to **Safari**
3. Tap **"Clear History and Website Data"**
4. Confirm by tapping **"Clear History and Data"**
5. Reopen Safari and visit the app

### For Desktop:
- **Chrome/Edge**: Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (Mac)
- **Firefox**: Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` or `Cmd+Shift+Delete`
- Select "Cached images and files" and clear

1. On the login screen, tap **"Continue as Guest"**
2. You'll get immediate access to all flashcards and scenarios
3. **Note:** Guest progress is NOT saved to an account

## ‚úÖ **Solution 4: Wait for Server to Wake Up**

If you see **"Connection timed out"** or **"Network error"**:

1. The server may be starting up (happens on first access after inactivity)
2. **Wait 10-15 seconds**
3. Tap the **"Try Again"** button or refresh the page
4. The app should load successfully on the second attempt

---

## üîç **Common Error Messages Explained:**

### "Network error. Please check your internet connection."
**Cause:** App can't reach the server  
**Fix:** Try Solutions 1, 2, and 4 above

### "Email already registered"
**Cause:** This email is already in use  
**Fix:** Use the **login page** instead of registration, or tap "Forgot Password" if you forgot your password

### "Connection timed out"
**Cause:** Server is starting up (takes 10-15 seconds on first access)  
**Fix:** Wait 15 seconds and try again

### "Password must be at least 6 characters"
**Cause:** Password is too short  
**Fix:** Create a password with 6 or more characters

### "Passwords do not match"
**Cause:** Your password and confirm password don't match  
**Fix:** Make sure both password fields have the exact same text
